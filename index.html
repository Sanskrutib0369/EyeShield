Eye Care First Aid

Eye Redness & Irritation

Cold Compress

Apply a clean, cold compress for 10 minutes to reduce inflammation

Artificial Tears

Use preservative-free artificial tears to soothe irritation

Avoid Rubbing

Resist rubbing your eyes as it may worsen the irritation

Dry Eyes

Use Humidifier

Add moisture to the air with a humidifier in your room

Blink Exercises

Practice complete blinks every 20 minutes while working

Lubricating Drops

Apply artificial tears or lubricating eye drops as needed

Foreign Object in Eye

Don't Rub

Avoid rubbing your eye as it may cause scratches

Flush with Water

Gently flush your eye with clean water for several minutes

Seek Help

If object persists, seek immediate medical attention

Digital Eye Strain

20-20-20 Rule

Every 20 minutes, look 20 feet away for 20 seconds

Screen Settings

Adjust brightness and contrast for comfortable viewing

Proper Position

Keep screen at arm's length and slightly below eye level
